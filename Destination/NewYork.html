How to Get There?

From the Philippines, you can fly to New York City via connecting flights offered by airlines such as Philippine Airlines, Cathay Pacific, and Qatar Airways. Flights typically connect through cities like Hong Kong, Doha, or Tokyo before arriving at John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK), LaGuardia Airport (LGA), or Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR).

Once in New York City, you can use the subway, buses, or taxis to get around. Ride-hailing apps like Uber and Lyft are also widely available.

Other Places to Visit in New York
